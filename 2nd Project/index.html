About our Restaurant

You are welcome for breakfast, lunch or dinner in our cosy restaurant. Our menu has a variety of delicious dishes. During the summer you can enjoy the sun on our terrace.

In our restaurant, we serve (almost) every afternoon a lunch buffet from 12.00 o’clock till 13.30 (except on Saturday and Sunday). On the buffet, you can find for example: The soup of the day, several meats, several cheeses, several types of bread, 6 kind of warm dishes (the dishes will change by day), several sweet fillings, several salads, coffee, tea, milk, buttermilk, orange juice and yoghurt. Price: €19.50 per person. It is also possible to lunch a la carte at our restaurant.
